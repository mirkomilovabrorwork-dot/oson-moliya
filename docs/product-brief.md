# PulTrack — Product Brief

PulTrack is built for Uzbekistan small-business owners — shopkeepers, traders, logistics operators — who currently track income and expenses in notebooks or WhatsApp chats and have no clear picture of where their money goes. The problem is that even simple bookkeeping apps require manual data entry through a form-heavy UI, which people avoid; voice or natural-language input via a messaging app they already use every day removes that friction entirely. PulTrack lets the owner send a text or voice message to a Telegram bot in Uzbek, Russian, or English ("logistikaga 500 ming chiqim"), and the bot's Claude-powered brain understands the amount, category, and intent, confirms the entry in seconds, and sends a dashboard button — so the owner opens a real multi-page web dashboard showing Overview charts, a searchable Transactions table, Analytics, and per-category budget limits with proactive overspend warnings, all without leaving Telegram or learning a new app. The key differentiator is the combination: natural-language input in three languages (including voice), a production-quality dashboard that shows meaningful analytics, and budget alerts that fire automatically inside the conversation — no cron jobs, no email reports, no app to remember to open.
